Galung Ga

A fearsome looking Ogre, with bright red eyes and a rather bright manner about him. He is an educated, street smart foreigner. Belonging to the Ogre tribes beyond the northern mountain, Kukthrakka . Galung Ga has seen a many things in his day's living a long and hardy life. Filled with tragedy, triumph, and most of all an appreciation for what he has. His age maybe showing but his mind and body have never been better. Some day he wishes to pay of his student loans.........
